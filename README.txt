File Formats:


actor_movies.txt
contains movies each actor has played in:

{actor1}\t\t{movie1}\t\t{movie2}\t\t{movie3}...
{actor2}\t\t{movie1}\t\t{movie2}\t\t{movie3}...
{actor3}\t\t{movie1}\t\t{movie2}\t\t{movie3}...
.
.
.




actress_movies.txt
contains movies each actress has played in:

{actress1}\t\t{movie1}\t\t{movie2}\t\t{movie3}...
{actress2}\t\t{movie1}\t\t{movie2}\t\t{movie3}...
{actress3}\t\t{movie1}\t\t{movie2}\t\t{movie3}...
.
.
.




director_movies.txt
contains movies of each director:

{director1}\t\t{movie1}\t\t{movie2}\t\t{movie3}...
{director2}\t\t{movie1}\t\t{movie2}\t\t{movie3}...
{director3}\t\t{movie1}\t\t{movie2}\t\t{movie3}...
.
.
.




movie_genres.txt
contains genre of each movie:

{movie1}\t\t{genre}
{movie2}\t\t{genre}
{movie3}\t\t{genre}
.
.
.




movie_rating.txt
contains rating of each movie:

{movie1}\t\t{rating}
{movie2}\t\t{rating}
{movie3}\t\t{rating}
.
.
.
